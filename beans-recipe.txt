Beans Recipe

1. Add four cups of water to a recipient.
2. Add 1 cup  of beans.
3. Add 1 spoon of Olive oil.
4. Have the water and beans under low heat for 2 hours - check accordingly, if they get dry, add 1/2 cup of water.
5. After 2 hours, add 1 clove of garlic.
6. After 20 mins, add 1/4 cup of Squash.
6. Have 1 clove of garlic, 1/2 pepper, 1/4 cup of celery and 1/4 of onions. Blend them.
7. After 30 mins, add one spoon of this mixture to the beans.
8. After 10 mins, get all the squash from the beans along with a little bit of beans and blend them.
9. Add this mixture back to the beans.
10. Add 1/2 teaspoon of oregano.
11. The beans are ready!
